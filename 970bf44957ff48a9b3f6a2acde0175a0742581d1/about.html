My name is Paulette Besa and I studied Graphic and Interactive Design at Valencia College in Orlando, Florida. A creative soul from the start, I always knew I wanted to be a designer. Through high school, I leaned towards becoming a Psychologist, but I always knew I wished to design experiences for others. This dream eventually evolved into my specializing in User Experience Design. I have an A.S in graphic and interactive design, and I am certified in User Experience. As a UX/UI Designer, I design and create websites and applications for everyday users, while mentoring students on the side.

Inspired by people and the ever-evolving way they interact with technology; I have always been fascinated by how our society views the world and the different ways technology and its conveniences affect those views. This curiosity pairs well with my interest in psychology. An avid reader, I can often be found with a psychology book, or biography, though I admit, I am Potterhead through and through.

Originally from Santiago, Chile, I live with my husband, and our fur baby Gnocchi, the orange tabby cat. As hikers, and nature enthusiasts, we wanted to be somewhere we could enjoy the mountains any time we wished. This is the reason why we moved to Denver, CO.

An avocado toast aficionado since childhood, I was overjoyed when the dish became the norm in the U.S. Now I no longer have to feel weird about loving it! I enjoy eating any food, preferably when its Ethiopian. Spotify is life, and I love dancing, drinking tea, playing guitar, singing, whether during a karaoke session or I am belting it out in the car.

I believe in helping, anyway and any time that I can, because even a small thing can make the world an easier place to live in for someone.
