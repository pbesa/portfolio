Case Study

Taylor Morrison

Taylor Morrison is a top U.S. homebuilder, specializing in crafting single-family homes and communities across multiple states. They prioritize innovative, sustainable living spaces tailored to homebuyers' needs and lifestyles. Known for quality craftsmanship and modern design, they prioritize customer satisfaction.

Role

  • Lead Designer
  • Lead Researcher

Problem

The main challenges were designing an integrated experience that effectively prioritizes essential information for users' decision-making, ensures seamless accessibility for users to connect with Taylor Morrison, and serves the needs of both external customers and internal stakeholders.

Solution

To achieve a seamless experience, we restructured pages, removed unpopular ones, consolidated those with minimal information, and prioritized key content for users. Simultaneously, we considered the needs of salespeople, who rely on the website for sales information.

Discovery

Usability Testing

We conducted an initial test of the website to identify user pain points and validate areas that were not functioning as intended. Concurrently, we held a focus group comprising marketing managers, sales representatives, and sales managers to gather insights into their usage patterns, pain points, and requirements.

Findings:

90%

of users struggled to find images and amenities.

40%

of users didn't like leaving the page for new information.

30%

of users had to call the sales office to ask information on how to navigate the website.

100%

of tested users were looking to buy a home, or had purchased a home within 12 months.

Focused group

Here are some quotes from internal users:

"Virtual tours/pictures and videos, should be easier to find."

Most people in the focused group agreed to this. They said that images are visual "hooks" that will engage people to read more.

"Usually people call about how to find specific information."

The pages were so poorly designed that people had to call to ask where to find information on the website.
