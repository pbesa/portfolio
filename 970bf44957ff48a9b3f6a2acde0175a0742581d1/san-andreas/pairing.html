What are tannins?

Tannins can come from many places, including the skins of the grapes used in winemaking as well as the wood barrels a wine may have been aged in. This astringent flavor is what helps strip the fats from your tongue and thereby cleanse the palate of the rich fats from a meal and provide a refined, refreshing drink.

The main concept behind pairings is that certain elements (such as texture and flavor) in both food and wine interact with each other, and thus finding the right combination of these elements will make the entire dining experience more enjoyable.
